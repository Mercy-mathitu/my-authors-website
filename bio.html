About Mathitu

I was born in a small village in Gatundu North, Kenya and raised in an equally small village in Gatundu South. I have a Bachelor’s Degree in Education (Arts) English and Literature. I am the middle child of three daughters and writing is at the top of my passions. I have loved reading and writing for as long as I can remember. I once exhausted the entire library in our primary school. I love reading stories so writing them was a no brainer for me, especially love stories with a happily ever after ending. I’m a sucker for romance. I wrote several stories which was how I bonded with my classmates in high school, some of whom still cheer me on to this day. I lost a lot of the stories I wrote then and in the few years after. Luckily for me, I’m always in my head, cooking up a story, keeping one going. I have never ran out, nor do I think I will.
